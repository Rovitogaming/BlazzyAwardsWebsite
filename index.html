What are the Blazzy Awards?

The Blazzy Awards is the funniest, most anticipated family event of the year! Every New Year's Eve, we gather together to celebrate the quirks, laughs, and unforgettable moments that make our family so special. From 'Most Middle Fingers Given Out' to 'Best Botox,' these awards capture the essence of who we are, all in good fun. So grab your popcorn, find your seat, and get ready for a night of laughter, love, and legendary Blazzy moments!
